Dr. Shawn Nouri

Artist Portfolio

Wamego, KS USA

Dr. Nouri, age 78, is a long time artist and Kansas resident. Born in Tehran, Iran, his work is inspired by surrealism and impressionism.

Gallery

Dr. Nouri's work is largely inspired by personal experiences of love, loss, family, and war.
